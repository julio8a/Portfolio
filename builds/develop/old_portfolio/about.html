Hello World, I’m Julio Ochoa…

I am a Graphic and Website Designer offering services in print and web. I live in Coachella, California and I will gladly do work here in my small town or anywhere in the United States. Hey I can take on the world, so where ever you are I will gladly work with you. I currently do freelance and enjoy working with people.

I attend the Art Institute of California - Inland Empire. I am in my sixth quarter and I am filling myself with knowledge. The will to learn and better myself has never been greater. I am someone who breathes design. I enjoy producing creative design material for a range of clients. On occasions, I like doing personal experimental projects to better myself in designing new and unique material. I love to keep up to date on the design world, because it is always changing, along with the web. Going on new adventures and learning as much as possible is always a good challenge for me.
