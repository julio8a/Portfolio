Save Me
Mobile App

Save me app provides organizations real time awareness of their employees and their status. The application notifies the mobile workers weather they are in a safe area, near a danger area or inside of a danger area. Save me app also provides workers with routing directions in case they are in or near a danger area, which will navigate them to safety.
